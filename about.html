I entered AUP, or the AMerican University of Paris, through the Trojan Transfer Plan. This means I get to study my first year abroad at Paris, and in my sophmore year I get to start attending the University of Southern California. This school has been such a blessing, as Paris is a beautiful city to not only live in, but is a great start to many different travel destinations.

I graduated Highschool in the year 2018. I graduated with 4 years of french, orchestra and honors. I completed the Ib program and was in the National Honor Society as well as the California Scholarship Federation.Highschool was a great time, but college was not too far behind!

Here I am at Prom with my two beautiful parents. they have supported me in everything I do, and I would not be able to travel or go on as many adventures as I was able to this summer. Thank you parents for supporting me in everything and giving me all the opportunities you never had the chance to take!

My parents immigrated to America but were both originally born in Belize. Each summer I go to visit family, and having the chance to visit has really opened up the love to travel in my heart.

Ever since I was small I've had an adventurous spirit. Here I am playing in a Belizian park, just hanging around looking for my next adventure.
