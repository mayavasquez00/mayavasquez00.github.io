Travel Hack #1

ALWAYS! Add an extra $100 (or more depending on the trip) into your budget. You never know when youre going to need to call an emergency Uber or will want to book a crazy experience youll regret not doing. Its better to work this 'safety' money into your budget from early, so that if you dont have to use it, its a nice treat to have some money left over.

One of my most important!
Travel Hack #2

When packing your luggage, its better to roll your clothes rather than fold them. This provides extra space, as well as keeping your clothes relatively wrinkle free! This is great when you bought more souvenirs than originaly planned.
Travel Hack #3

Pack bottles conatining liquids into extra large and durable ziploc bags. This ensures, if any bottles are broken open, or some liquid seems to squeeze out for any reason, they will not spread onto clothes or anything else.

Travel Hack #4

Create a mini 'emergency' bag for each trip. This includes, chapstick, hair ties, a lock and key, eye mask, etc.This are little luxuries easily forgotten, but will make all the difference on a long journey.
Travel Hack #5

Only use cash while traveling. Most banques charge a fee for every ATM withdrawl and transaction, get around this by pulling majority of cash at once. It is also important to note that one should keep different amounts of cash in seperate places. If wandering hands get ahold if one, youll still have some safety cash.

Travel Hack #6

Hotels can get very expensive very fast.A good alternative would be websites such as Airbnb. Live like a local in the comfort of well reviewed and ensured homes.
Travel Hack #7

Although dowloading an extra app onto your phone seems like a wast of storage, it can actually be quite helpful. Having your airlines app installed can have paperless boarding and helpful info about boarding information and flight arival time.
Travel Hack #8

Make copies of your passport, print and email these. This could be a life saver if you happen to misplace yours for any reason.

Travel Hack #9

Buy a reusable bottle! This not only saves the planet, but it will end up saving you lots of money as well.
Travel Hack #10

Remember to have fun! When your traveling, it can be quite stressful and will often get the best of people at times.
